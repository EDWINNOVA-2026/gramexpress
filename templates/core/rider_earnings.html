Workflow

How this earnings model works

Step 1. Earn commission first

Every completed delivery adds Rs. {{ delivery_fee_per_order }} to your rider commission total.

Step 2. Clear the 20% gate

Once your delivered-order share reaches {{ completion_share_threshold }}% of the tracked platform pool, fixed-pay support becomes available.

Step 3. Support fills the gap

If your commission is below Rs. {{ earnings_goal }}, the app adds a top-up capped at Rs. {{ fixed_pay_cap }}.

Step 4. Bonus rewards over-performance

If your commission itself crosses Rs. {{ earnings_goal }}, the top-up is skipped and the goodwill bonus of Rs. 1000 is shown instead.
